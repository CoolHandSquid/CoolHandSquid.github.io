Mississippi

1. Download zip file from here

2. Unzip the file.

3. Open Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions

4. In the top right corner click on the toggle to enable "developer mode"

5. In the upper left corner click "Load Unpacked"

6. In the windows that pops up navigate to the Mississippi file you unzipped, click "Select Folder"

7. Open another tab in chrome and navigate to amazon.com

8. Go to your shopping cart and you'll be among the first to see Mississippi
